## Backlog

## Multi-Character Tray and Recents

Let people collect characters into a scratchpad, reorder them, copy the full string in raw or escaped forms, and revisit recent characters quickly.

## Related, Confusable, and Paired Characters

Show practical alternatives and nearby characters for quotes, dashes, spaces, arrows, and common lookalikes.

## Structured Filters

Add first-class filters for kind, category, script, block, age, and hidden classes to support broader Unicode exploration.

## Shareable URLs

Encode the current query, selected character, or future tray contents in the URL so a specific state can be shared and reopened.

## Multi-Code-Point Sequences

Support modern Unicode sequences such as ZWJ emoji, flags, skin-tone variants, variation selectors, and named sequences.

## CJK and Unihan Enrichment

Add deeper Han support with definitions, readings, radicals, stroke counts, and stronger CJK-specific discovery workflows.

## Offline PWA Polish

Extend the mobile-friendly app into a stronger offline experience with more complete caching and app-like behavior.

## Font-Related Tooling

Explore optional font-focused features such as coverage checks, fallback inspection, and deeper glyph rendering diagnostics.
