# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Charmap2 to be an open, respectful, and constructive project for everyone.

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- being respectful in tone and disagreement
- assuming good intent while still giving clear technical feedback
- accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- focusing on what is best for the project and its users
- helping newcomers understand context when possible

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- trolling, personal attacks, or inflammatory comments
- public or private intimidation
- publishing others' private information without permission
- other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this policy.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is officially representing the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it through the security/contact channel described in `SECURITY.md`. Reports will be reviewed and handled as promptly as possible.

## Consequences

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate in response to unacceptable behavior, including warning, temporary restriction, or permanent ban from the project.
